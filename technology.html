01 / Laser

Laser Hair Removal

Medical-grade laser energy

Targeted pulses of light are absorbed by the pigment in the hair follicle, heating and disabling it at the root while leaving surrounding skin unharmed. Integrated cooling keeps each pulse comfortable. Because hair grows in cycles, a series of sessions delivers the smoothest, longest-lasting result — on the face and across the body.

CoolingComfort-first pulses
SeriesCycle-based plan
02 / Microneedling

SkinPen Microneedling

FDA-cleared collagen induction

SkinPen creates thousands of precise micro-channels in the skin, triggering the body's natural wound-healing cascade. The result is fresh collagen and elastin that refine texture, soften fine lines, and improve the look of pores and scarring. As the first FDA-cleared microneedling device, it's a clinical standard for safe, controlled resurfacing.

FDACleared device
Single-useSterile cartridge
03 / Energy

RF Microneedling

Radiofrequency + microneedling

This combines microneedling with radiofrequency energy delivered through the needle tips, heating the deeper dermis where collagen lives. The dual action tightens lax skin, smooths texture, and resurfaces — reaching depths a needle alone can't — making it a powerful option for firmness, scarring, and overall rejuvenation with minimal downtime.

DualNeedle + RF heat
DeepDermal remodeling
04 / Delivery

MicronJet

Micro-channel infusion

MicronJet uses ultra-fine, micro-engineered channels to deliver targeted treatment solutions into the skin precisely and comfortably. By placing actives exactly where they're needed in a controlled, shallow plane, it maximizes absorption and effect while keeping the experience gentle — a refined alternative to traditional injection delivery.

PreciseControlled depth
GentleMicro-fine channels
05 / Biostimulation

PRX Biostimulators

No-needle bio-revitalization

PRX is a topical biostimulator that revitalizes the skin without needles or downtime. Applied and massaged into the surface, it stimulates the deeper layers to produce new collagen — firming, brightening, and restoring radiance. It pairs beautifully with microneedling and is ideal for clients who want a glow-up with no recovery period.

No-needleZero downtime
CollagenDeep stimulation

Customized acid resurfacing

Professional-grade peels use carefully selected acids to gently exfoliate the outermost skin, accelerating cell turnover. The result is a brighter, more even tone, refined texture, and a renewed barrier. Every formula and strength is chosen for your skin type and goals — from a luminous refresh to a deeper correction.
