SkinVerse Laser and Wellness was founded on a simple belief: great skin care should be clinical, personal, and free of the pressure that defines so much of the industry.

Our story

Founded in 2025, built on trust.

SkinVerse opened its doors in St. Petersburg with a clear purpose — to bring registered-nurse-led, science-backed aesthetics to a city that deserves better than guesswork and hard sells.

Every treatment we offer earns its place on the menu. We choose proven technology, follow clinical protocols, and take the time to understand your skin before we ever recommend a thing. The result is care that feels less like a transaction and more like a partnership.

We're proudly independent, intentionally focused, and entirely committed to results you can see and feel.

What we stand for

Our principles.

Three commitments shape every consultation, every treatment, and every recommendation we make.

01

An RN oversees your care from consultation through aftercare, so every decision is grounded in medical knowledge — not trends.
